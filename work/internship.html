Philosophy Outreach Project Website

I also worked with an immersive learning class from the Philosophy department at Ball State this semester. The class works to introduce high school students in the study of philosophy through online resources, school visits and an annual conference. They had a website(link) with most of the information, but came to the Corps to get a more customized and comprehensive online presence that complies with the Ball State brand guidelines.

The main design feature of the site is the custom illustrations on every page. While pitching her vision to us, the client showed several websites that all had custom-drawn elements on them. The team picked up on this and decided it was fitting for the target audience of high school students to break out of the typical minimalistic design.
